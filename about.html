My Biography
I completed my secondary studies in 2022, focusing on mathematics,
economics, and computer science. After high school, I began coding
and had the opportunity to engage in game development at the ICT
Innovation Center Kigali Hub, where we used C# and Unity to create
mobile and desktop games. I worked in game development for about
seven months while also exploring web development.
Currently, I am pursuing a degree in software engineering
at African Leadership University. I have an experience
in HTML, CSS, Python, JavaScript (Node.js), C#,
and MySQL, with some foundational knowledge in Java and C++.
Looking ahead, I plan to specialize in Artificial Intelligence.
